About Hackathon

Hackathon is being organised by Indian Road Safety Campaign and Livemedia in association with Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. It aims to utilise the technical skills of students to improve the road safety scenario in India. Let's code for a safer India and experience the unexpected learning and fun. Win a lot, Learn a lot.

What is Our Goal?

The aim of this hackathon is to develop an app or web based solution to a given road-safety problem involving the best minds of premier institutes of the country.

  • Learn from the experts of the field
  • Get a chance to present your project at a national platform
  • Release your product/solution into main market stream

Themes for Projects

  • Driver Behaviour Recognition and Improvement:

    The participants can develop webapps or mobile apps that can measure various facets of a driver's driving habits and notify him/her of the same.

  • Identifying and Notifying Driving Distractions:

    Driver distractions like mobile phone usage, loud music, etc. may be detected and presented to the driver in an innovative way as well.

  • Detecting Fraudulent Drivers:

    Any technical solution that can be used to check whether or not a person driving a vehicle is eligible.

Note: These are only the starter points for the projects and we expect the students to come up with their own innovative ideas surrounding solutions to road safety related problems.

Rounds

The event is divided into 3 round. It is compulsory for each team to attend a session on the “Role of Technology in Road-Safety” which will be held between 5-7:00 pm in LHC, IIT Delhi.Each team need to submit a write up containing the problem and idea of solution.These problems will lie in 3 domains- driver behaviour, road conditions and fraud detection.The participating teams need to submit their solution(in Max 100 words) of any of the problem before 8:00 pm, 16th Feb’18

Round 1

  • This will be a 36 hr competition in which the team are required to develop a prototype of an app/web based solution of the problem that they have chosen.
  • Top 50 teams from the registered will be eligible for this.
  • A set of experts will be available, from which the teams can take help both in technical and road-safety aspects.
  • There will be a training session on 17th of 2 hour each from 12:00pm - 6:00pm , each team needs to attend one of these session. The teams need to submit their code along with a power-point presentation of the idea before 18th Feb’18 11am.
  • Top 10 teams will be awarded and selected for round 2.

Round 2

  • Each team will be allotted a mentor and they will have to develop their model over a span of 1 month.
  • 5 teams will be selected in this round.

Round 3

  • The work of the selected teams will be presented to the Ministry of Road Transport and Highway(MoRTH).
  • The team will continue to work on their project for another 1 months under the surveillance of LiveMedia.
  • The selected idea will be taken forward as a startup by Live Media.
